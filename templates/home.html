What is JobCentric?

JobCentric is a site dedicated to bringing transparency to both the hiring and applying process

Who's JobCentric for?

JobCentric is built for both employers and job seekers. JobCentric does all the heavy lifting on both ends, keeping users updated on a job listing or application.

Why should I use JobCentric

JobCentric is built on one foundational principal - no person filling out a job application or an employeer deserves to feel unsure in where they stand. With a system built in place to both manage and track the status of job listings, applications, and other key parts that play a role in hiring/applying, JobCentric can make applying to jobs a little less stressful.
